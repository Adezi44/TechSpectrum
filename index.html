Making your sprite talk!

Let's do some math!

🔁 3. Loops (Repeat Actions)

Make things happen over and over!

move 10 steps
forever

🤔 4. Conditional Statements (If-Then)

Making smart decisions!
if key space pressed? then
say Space pressed!
if touching mouse-pointer? then
change size by 10
else
change size by -5
